Title: About Olle

----

Text: 

Olle Enqvist has six years of professional  experience under his belt. Hailing from Nättraby, in southern Sweden, Olle is fond of cats, hardcore music, DJing, and bäss.

Olle started with photography at the ripe young age of 14, which quickly led to him shooting at local rock concerts and beyond. Olle became active shooting for Rockfoto.nu in 2010.
